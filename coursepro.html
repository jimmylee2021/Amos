Course Pro Content Redesign

Role: UX Writer/Content Strategist

Collaborators: UI Designer, Product Manager,

Background

CoursePro is an online education platform for busy professionals to learn at their own pace. The platform also allows course creators to upload their courses and earn money.

I was tasked with redesigning the content on this page and improving the microcopies especially. My first action was to review old content to determine which needs an improvement or not. I then researched competitors like Domestika, Coursera and Udemy. I discovered that although these brands were creative with their copies, they were very concise with the copies and ambiguous copies like "get started" were absent. I also realised that Course Pro has a lot of grammatical and punctuation mistakes all of which I corrected in the content redesign. I redesigned the copies with the intention to solve the highlighted problems in mind. I have also included notes on the reason for my word choices and other actions.
